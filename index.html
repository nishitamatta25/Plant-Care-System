Starting Your Medicinal Herb Garden

1

Choose Your Location

Most medicinal herbs need at least 6 hours of sunlight per day. Select a spot with good drainage and access to water.

2

Start with Easy Plants

Begin with hardy, easy-to-grow herbs like peppermint, chamomile, and lemon balm that are forgiving for beginners.

3

Prepare Your Soil

Medicinal herbs thrive in well-draining soil. Mix garden soil with compost and sand if needed for better drainage.

4

Planting & Spacing

Follow spacing guidelines for each plant. Some herbs spread aggressively and need containment.

5

Regular Care

Water when soil feels dry, prune regularly to encourage growth, and watch for pests.
